The Screen Time Chronicles: A Journey Through My Digital Habits

In a world where our lives are increasingly intertwined with digital devices, I decided to embark on a journey to understand my relationship with my phone. Armed with screen time tracking tools, I set out to uncover the patterns, habits, and perhaps even the hidden truths behind my daily screen usage. What started as a simple curiosity soon turned into a fascinating exploration of my digital life.

Where is this data from?

For one month, I constantly tracked my screen time, then organizing each session into different categories: Social Media, Entertainment, Productivity and Other. The data that I gathered using my phone's built-in tracker. Being able to have access to this extensive data allowed me to analyze my screen time habits and patterns, and see where all the time has gone.

The first step was to gather all the data I needed. After ignoring my phone's screen time tracker for years, this was the time to pay more attention to it. Everyday I monitored my screen time, and organized them in their own categories: Social Media, Entertainment, Productivity and Other. Although I do not remember letting them have this data (I am sure I agreed to it at some point), as time went on I grew to appreciate it. When it came down to it, I was able to see how much time I spent on my phone, and where all that time went. These numbers proved to me that I have more phone habits than I thought I did. Boredom? Stress? Inflation? Anxiety? (or all at the same time!) No wonder I was always tired. I was always on my phone. Now it's time to really display this data so I can get it together!
